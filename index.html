Happy Birthday, Mollie

Today is your day, and even if I’m not with you physically, I’m with you in every other way.

You’re one of the kindest, deepest, and most genuine people I’ve ever known.

You know how to listen. You feel things deeply. You love with presence. And you know how to be there for people like no one else.

I want you to know how proud I am of you. Proud of the person you are.

You’re not just smart and beautiful — you’re strong and real.

You manage to be a daughter, a friend, a girlfriend, a student — and still stay true to yourself.

You hold so much warmth in you, and you never force it.

You don’t just live — you bring meaning. That’s your power.

You made my day special just by inviting me to that concert and spending the evening with me. And thank you for your letter. It was real. Just like you.

On your birthday, I want to wish you one thing: to be truly happy. Not the loud kind of happy. The quiet, peaceful, real kind.

I hope you’re always surrounded by people who see in you what I see — people who love you not for what you do, but for who you are.

You mean so much to me. And I’m grateful for everything we’ve shared — and everything still ahead.

— Eldar
